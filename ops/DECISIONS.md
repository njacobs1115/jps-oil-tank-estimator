# Decisions Log
> Append new entries at the top. Never delete old entries.
> Every decision has a STATUS. Format: date, decision, rationale, status.

---

## STATUS KEY
- `PROPOSED` — recommended, awaiting approval
- `APPROVED` — approved, not yet executed
- `LIVE` — executed, measuring
- `MEASURING` — changes live, watching data
- `CLOSED` — outcome confirmed
- `DEFERRED` — not acted on, reason documented
- `REJECTED` — declined, reason documented
